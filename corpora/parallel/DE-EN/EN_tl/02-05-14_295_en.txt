No, Mr Papayannakis, the Commission does not have any such proposals and I must make it quite clear that I would have great concerns about agreeing to the idea that the Roma issue should be 'Europeanised' by treating Roma as a transnational European minority with their own European minority status.
No, I believe that what we need to do here is to urge the Member States and future Member States, using all the means at our disposal, to vigorously combat the problem of discrimination, and by means of Community programmes we can help to support that fight, which is just what we are doing.
