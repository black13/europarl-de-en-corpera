I agree in principle with Paolo Costa's report on civil aviation.
It is important for the EU and the US to agree on a common line with the help of this agreement.
However, it is vital in this overseas partnership that we are real partners, not just on paper.
Criteria must be found to which both parties must adhere.
In the event of contempt on the part of either the EU or the US, a rescindable variation in the agreement will be indispensable.
