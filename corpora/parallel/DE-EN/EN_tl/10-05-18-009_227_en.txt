Education is important, but the reality is that a good education and training has, for quite some time, been no guarantee of employment, let alone a good job.
Instead of investing money in appropriate qualification schemes, the plan is to ship lowly qualified staff into the EU using all possible kinds of card solutions, with transitional deadlines in the labour market to be dismissed as unnecessary.
In the context of the basic conditions referred to, the training programme is not adequate, for which reason I voted 'no'.
